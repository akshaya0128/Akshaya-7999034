Exercise 12.1 – Margin & Padding Utilities

Bootstrap spacing utilities follow the pattern {property}{sides}-{size}.
Property: m (margin) or p (padding).
Sides: t top, b bottom, s start, e end, x horizontal, y vertical.
Size: 0 to 5 or auto.
